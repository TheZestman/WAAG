Ragnarok is Coming this November!



After what seems like an eternity(twas only 4 years) we finally not only have more information on the sequel to 2018's game of the year but we also actually have a confirmed release date! God Of War Ragnarök is slated to release this fall on November 9th. This release was accompanied by an approximately 30 second CGI trailer that sets the mood for what we will experience this fall. Several different versions of the game will be available for purchase when it releases. The standard ps4/ps5 editions will come with only the game. The digital deluxe edition comes with a wealth of in-game content as well as the digital soundtrack and a digital artbook. The physical collector’s edition will end up being quite the purchase for fans. This comes with a special steel display case, 2 carved twin figures, a replica of a shrine and last but not least, an actual replica of the hammer Mjolnir! This alone will have me eyeing the collector’s edition! This is as cool as Fallout 4 releasing with an actual Pip-Boy(if you know you know) which was also incredible. God of War(2018) is widely acclaimed as one of the best games of the last console situation so Ragnarök will have some very big shoes to fill.
